What Is Meowdoku?

Meowdoku is a cat-themed placement puzzle for players who like Sudoku, Queens, Star Battle, and color-region logic games. The goal is simple to read and satisfying to solve: place one cat in every colored room while keeping every row, column, and neighbor space calm.

You may know it as Meowdoku, Meow Doku, meow sudoku, cat sudoku, cat queens puzzle, or even misspellings like meowdokou. This web version is built so you can play instantly in the browser.

Quick Rules

  1. Each colored room gets exactly one cat.
  2. No two cats can share a row or column.
  3. Cats cannot touch, including diagonally.

Meowdoku Strategy

Meowdoku gets easier when you start with rooms that have only a few possible cells, then use row and column pressure to remove choices. Assist mode shows the quiet exclusion dots around placed cats, which is useful while learning.

Meowdoku Levels

Meowdoku easy boards are 6x6, normal boards are 7x7, and hard boards are 8x8. Every difficulty keeps three hearts so the challenge comes from logic, not punishment.
